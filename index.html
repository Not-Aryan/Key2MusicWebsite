Key2Music

We're a student run non-profit organization
which seeks to provide accessible music
education through primarily string
instruments to children.

Donate

Key2Music relies on donations in order to fund instruments for children. Please make a donation to Key2Music with the GoFundMe link below. We also have frequent fundraisers!

Chapters

Our chapters each provide the resources of Key2Music to their respective regions. Chapter teams organize workshops, lessons, and camps with the guidance of the executive team.

View Page

Volunteer

Volunteers make up the heart of Key2Music. Our volunteers teach lessons to underprivileged children, lead workshops, and participate in our fundraisers.

Hungry For Music is an organization that provides String instruments for our students to use.

Sono Strings is a community oriented String Shop that helps Key2Music through providing professional guidance, and repair of string instruments.
